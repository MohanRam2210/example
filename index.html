South Africa further solidified themselves as one of the strong contenders
in 2023 ICC Cricket World Cup tournament after a convincing win
over five-time winners Australia on Thursday (October 12).

Great bowling display from African pacers, backed by Quinton de Kock's

thumping 134-run victory over Australia in their ICC Cricket World Cup
match in Lucknow. The loss against South Africa is a big blow to Australia's
